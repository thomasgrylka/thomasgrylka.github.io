About Me

This is who I am.

Thomas (Tom) Grylka loves programming, writing, photography, and hanging out with his dog. He does not love talking about himself in the third person. He is presently employed as a remote agent at Finalsite, providing exceptional technical helpdesk support to international clients. Previously, he worked as a QA analyst for test.io and as a freelance content developer on UpWork.com. Also, he got his BGS (Bachelor of General Studies) at Eastern Connecticut State University.
